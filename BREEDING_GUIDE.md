# 🐉 Monster Breeding Simulator - Complete Breeding Guide

This guide explains how breeding works in detail, including all the deep mechanics we've built.

## How to Breed

1. Run the game: `python3 monster_breeder.py`
2. In the GUI, select **two monsters** from the list (hold Ctrl to multi-select).
3. Click **BREED SELECTED**.
4. Read the detailed procedural description.
5. If conception succeeds, the female becomes **pregnant**.
6. Use the **Advance Gestation & Birth** button to progress pregnancies and trigger births.

## Key Mechanics

### 1. Genital Traits
Monsters can have 0-2 heritable traits that dramatically affect breeding:

- **Prominent Knot / Inflatable Knot**: Creates a strong lock during climax. Traps cum inside and forces it deep into the womb.
- **Backward Barbs**: Adds extra stimulation and grip.
- **Flared Head, Ridge Pattern, Dual Shafts, Prehensile**, etc.

Traits are passed to offspring with a chance of mutation.

### 2. Knot Size Variation (1-5)
- Knot size is determined by **Strength + Vitality** + randomness.
- Bigger knots = more intense stretching, stronger seal, and more dramatic belly swelling.
- Knot size is **heritable** with mutation.
- Shown in the monster list as `[Large Knot]`, `[Massive Knot]`, etc.

### 3. Cum Volume (Tracked)
- Every male has a tracked cum volume (roughly 30-150+ units).
- Higher volume = more likely to overfill the female and cause visible belly swelling.

### 4. Womb Capacity
- Females have a womb capacity (40-200+ units).
- When the male's load exceeds ~80% of her capacity while knotted, she experiences:
  - Visible belly swelling
  - Some overflow around the knot
- Bigger womb capacity = can handle heavier loads more comfortably.

### 5. Pregnancy & Birth System
- Successful breeding sets the female as pregnant.
- Litter size is based on **female's fertility + father's cum volume**.
- Use **Advance Gestation & Birth** to progress pregnancies.
- When a pregnancy reaches 100%, birth occurs and the expected number of offspring are added to your stable.

### 6. Full Monster Descriptions
Click **Inspect** on any monster to see:
- Full stats
- Knot size
- Pregnancy status
- Detailed (very lewd) genitalia + vagina descriptions
- Pregnancy flavor text

## Tips for Better Results

- Pair high-fertility females with high-cum-volume males for bigger litters.
- Monsters with **knot traits** generally result in more reliable conception because the knot acts as a plug.
- Advance gestation multiple times after breeding to trigger births.
- Inspect pregnant monsters to see how far along they are.
- Cross-species breeding can produce interesting hybrid offspring.
